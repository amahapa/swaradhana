# tanpura_1 — Sample Attribution

Tanpura drone recordings in this directory originate from **Rāga Junglism**
(https://ragajunglism.org/tools/tanpura/), which states the resources are
"100% open-access and ad-free." No formal Creative Commons tag is published,
so they are redistributed here under a good-faith attribution arrangement.

Please retain this file and credit Rāga Junglism in any public listing of the
app's third-party assets. If Swaradhana is ever distributed commercially,
contact the site owner to confirm redistribution terms in writing.

## Files

- `pa_sa_{c,db,d,eb,e,f,gb,g,ab,a,bb,b}.mp3` — Pa-Sa tuning (1st string = Pa of
  mandra saptak; 2nd & 3rd = Sa of madhya saptak; 4th = Sa of mandra saptak)
- `ma_sa_{c,db,d,eb,e,f,gb,g,ab,a,bb,b}.mp3` — Ma-Sa tuning (1st string = Ma of
  mandra saptak; other strings as above)

## Pending

Ni-Sa tuning is not yet included here. A matched 12-key CC-BY set has not been
